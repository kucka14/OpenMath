What is OpenMath.US?
OpenMath.US is a completely free math education site for teachers and learners of all ages. It has a Question Collection of great math questions and a series of self-guided lessons you can assign to your students.
Or click here to find out what makes us different.
{% include 'lesson/chunks/index_carousel.html' %}
Each self-guided student lesson provides quantitative and qualitative student progress, in an easy-to-view format.
All content is carefully curated to be pedagogically and mathematically rich. Don't waste time vetting material.
Each unit and lesson is carefully constructed to build upon previous lessons, starting with the fundamentals.
Intuitive Lessons
Set up students to succeed with intuitive, engaging lessons.
Each lesson contains interactive, visual puzzles that make learning fun and addictive. Get started.
Data Informed Instruction
Too many students fall through the cracks. Use our self-guided curriculum to track how your students are doing, knowing that they'll always have engaging work to do. Get started.
Get Started Today
What are you waiting for? Explore our collection of free math resources, or create an account to start assigning your students work right away.
We Are a Community
This is a community endeavor. Please email us at openmath.us@gmail.com with suggestions, critiques, or to find out how to get involved. Read more about us here.

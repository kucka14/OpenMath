About OpenMath.US

Hi, my name is Andy Kuck, and I am the creator of OpenMath.US. I am a middle school math teacher. At one point or another, I have taught math at almost every level, from kindergarten to college. My mission is to make learning (and teaching) math as fun and enriching and un-tedius as it should be.

Everywhere I taught, I came across two recurring problems.

Problem 1: Finding Great Math Questions. Finding great math questions was tedious. I often found myself clicking through dozens of websites to find the question or worksheet that was just right for my students. I knew there had to be a better way.

Problem 2: Differentiating For Everyone. I always had students far below grade level, and students far above grade level. Despite my best efforts, I could never differentiate effectively for all my students. Too often, some students were confused, and some students were bored. I wanted to meet the needs of all my students, but I didn't know how.

Creating OpenMath.US
OpenMath.US has two primary features: a large question repository and a self-guided curriculum.

The Question Collection
Large repository of great math questions.
Problems added daily, for all grade levels.
Your one-stop shop for enriching math problems.

The Self-Guided Curriculum
Series of interactive lessons that starts at the very beginning.
Distraction-free and mathematically rich.
Accessible to the most struggling students, and engaging for the most advanced.
The perfect activity to supplement what you do in the classroom.

Make OpenMath.US Better

I hope you check out the site, and try it in your own classroom. I wouldn’t be putting this online if I didn’t find it personally useful. However, every teacher has different needs. If you want to be involved, please email us at openmath.us@gmail.com.

Give me suggestions, critiques, whatever. I want this to be a community effort. If you have a great resource on ratios you want included, I will include it. If you want the self-guided curriculum to have a lesson on negative fractions, I will create and add a lesson on negative fractions. If something doesn’t make sense, or if you would like a new feature added, just let me know.

Lesson Creation Guarantee: If you would like a lesson topic added to our self-guided curriculum, simply email us at openmath.us@gmail.com. I will add it to the site within one week so that you can start using it with your students.
